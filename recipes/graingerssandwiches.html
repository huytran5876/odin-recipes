Charlie Graingers Sandwiches

Description

Recipes for Charlie Grainger's Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Hoagie Roll
  • Hotdog Bun
  • Smoked and Cooked Brisket
  • Smoked and Cooked Pork Shoulder
  • Charlie's Sweet Molasses Sauce
  • Charlie's Spicy Vinegar Sauce
  • Chili
  • Cheese
  • Pimento Cheese
  • Jamaican Relish
  • Jalapenos
  • Cole Slaw
  • Pickles
  • Okra

Steps

  1. Steam Hoagie Roll for about three seconds
  2. Prepare choice of meat while roll(s) are steaming
  3. Add sauce to own liking
  4. Add choice of toppings

Charlie Graingers Sandwiches Cheat Sheet

Every sandwich topped with cole slaw, pickles, and okra.
