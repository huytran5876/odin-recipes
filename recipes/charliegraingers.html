Charlie Graingers HotDogs

Description

Recipes from plain hotdogs to signature hotdogs.

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs
  • Hotdog buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Chili
  • Cole Slaw
  • Diced Onions
  • Sauerkraut
  • Jamaican Relish

Steps

  1. Boil hotdogs
  2. Once boiled, place hotdogs in hotdog buns
  3. Steam hotdogs and buns together for about three seconds
  4. Add toppings to own likings.

Charlie Graingers Hotdogs Cheat Sheet

  • Old Fashioned - Mustard, Chili, Onions
  • Charlie Graingers - Mustard, Chili, Cole Slaw
  • All the Way - Mustard, Chili, Onions, Slaw
  • S Double J - Ketchup, Mustard
  • Wildcat - Ketchup, Mustard, Chili
  • Easy Street - Mustard, Sauerkraut
  • Cape Fear - Mustard, Chili, Sauerkraut
  • Jamaican - Mustard, Jamaican Relish, Onions
